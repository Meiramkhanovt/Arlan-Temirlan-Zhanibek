About our project

«SunShine» - a unique institution. The concept of our menu is based on two principles: honesty and simplicity. Since 2022, our guests have been enjoying different types of sweets, which we prepare only from fresh and high-quality products.

Each dish - from classic recipes to current novelties - is distinguished by high quality, concise and attractive serving. Visit us and try it yourself.
